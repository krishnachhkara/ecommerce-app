-------------Product Upload/Create flow -----------

1. Validate images
2. Validate primary index

3. Upload images to ImageKit
      ↓
   Collect URLs

4. Create Product
      ↓
   db.add(product)
   await db.flush()

5. Now product.id exists

6. Create ProductImage rows
      ↓
   product_id=product.id

7. commit


----------------Flow-------------------

Validate
    ↓
Upload Images
    ↓
Collect URLs
    ↓
Create Product
    ↓
flush()
    ↓
product.id available
    ↓
Create ProductImages
    ↓
commit()
    ↓
refresh(product)
    ↓
return product
